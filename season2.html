This is for the auidence who has watched Season 1 of Shadow and Bones.

Recap of what happened in Season 1

According to USA Today, "In Season 1, Alina discovers her dormant powers and is swept off to the capital of her country, Ravka (a Russia-like nation), by the powerful General Kirigan to be trained to destroy the Shadow Fold, which splits Ravka in two. By the end of the season, Alina discovers Kirigan's unsavory intentions for her powers" (Lawlor, 2023).

What is expected in Season 2?

"The second season of Shadow and Bone will see the Darkling emerge from his near-death experience in the Fold with a terrifying new power, the ability to create and control shadow monsters known as nichevo’ya using merzost, the power of creation," according to Times (McCluskey, 2023).
